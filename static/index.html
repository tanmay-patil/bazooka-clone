What is Bazonga?

Bazonga is a platform for playing real‑time, collaborative mini games, bringing friends and strangers together across devices. Whether you’re at home, commuting or hosting a party, Bazonga delivers instant fun.

How it works

Simply connect with others to play multiplayer games instantly – no downloads or installations required. Invite your friends or join others and start the fun in seconds. Bazonga runs right in your browser on mobile, tablet and desktop.

Privacy features

Bazonga is designed with privacy in mind. No personal data is ever stored or tracked, and you can play anonymously without logging in. Your gaming sessions are transient and leave no footprint behind.

No Database: How Bazonga keeps things simple

Without using any database, Bazonga remains straightforward and easy to use. Just choose your game, connect with others and start playing – no accounts, no hassles and nothing to remember.
